Arcaea

Arcaea is a rhythm video game developed and published by the British company Lowiro. The game was released on iOS and Android mobile platforms on 9 March 2017. An offline version of the game was released for the Nintendo Switch on 18 May 2021.

maimai

Maimai is an arcade rhythm game series developed and distributed by Sega, in which the player interacts with objects on a touchscreen and executes dance-like movements. The game supports both single player and multiplayer gameplay with up to 2 players per cab.

(Website)

Dead by Daylight

Dead by Daylight is an asymmetrical 4v1 multiplayer horror game developed by Behaviour Interactive. One player takes on the role of a brutal Killer hunting in the first-person, while four others play as Survivors attempting to escape in the third-person.

(Website)

VALORANT

Valorant is a free-to-play, 5v5 competitive tactical first-person shooter (FPS) developed by Riot Games. It blends precise, lethal gunplay with character-specific abilities, where teams of attackers and defenders alternate roles in a race to win 13 rounds using strategy, teamwork, and economic management.
